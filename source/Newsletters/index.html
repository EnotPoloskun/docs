SendGrid's Marketing Email tool is a powerful web and API based interface that allows you to compose and send scheduled marketing campaigns from your SendGrid account. Marketing Emails can be split tested, lists can be managed, and recipients can manage their subscriptions. You can upload your newsletter to facebook or twitter directly from SendGrid, and much more!

For information on programmatically interfacing with SendGrid newsletters, check out the Marketing Email API documentation.

{% info %} Sending Marketing Emails is currently available for the Silver Plan and above. An additional charge may be applied to newsletter sends. See the Pricing Page for more info. {% endinfo %}
