WordPress

To send email through SendGrid using WordPress we recommend WP SendGrid. To install, use the WordPress admin panel or follow these instructions. Once installed, click on the Settings link for the plugin on your plugins page, enter your SendGrid username and password, and you’ll be ready to go!
