Click Tracking

Enabling the Click Tracking App causes all the links in your emails to be overwritten and pointed to our servers so we can track clicks. If you would like a custom name to appear in the links you can create a CNAME that points to sendgrid.net and fill out the Whitelabel settings (only available in Silver or higher accounts) in your account with your new name.

Settings

Name Required Description
Enable Yes All the links in your emails will be overwritten, shortened and pointed to our servers.
